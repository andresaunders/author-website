More Than Conquerors

Christisans throughout the ages, have lived out these words spoken by the Apostle Paul, and have gained the victory over sin and the deception of the world by living a life hid in Messiah. But for the 144,000s, these words have special meaning, as their victory wll be acheived during the most troubled season in earth's history.
The question then is, what is it about the 144,000s that will enable them to stand during this trying time? What sets their character apart? What characer flaws will they have overcome? How will they overcome the divison caused by racism and the legacy of the Plantation System and become one in the multicultural body of Christ?
In this book, Dr. Saunders addresses these questions and more, and through the lens of prophecy, bliblcal number symbolism, and a study of Messianic Israel, he hopes to inspire the reader to more closely examine their hearts, and to place their life more fully in the hands of Christ, the "hope of glory".
